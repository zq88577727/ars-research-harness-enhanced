# GBD CVD China-Global Summary

Using the targeted GBD 2023 export, cardiovascular disease burden was summarized for China and the global population in 1990 and 2023.

In China, all-age CVD deaths changed from 3,303,961.86 (UI 2,797,927.14-3,795,995.79) in 1990 to 4,501,842.59 (UI 3,855,077.51-5,013,029.84) in 2023, while all-age CVD DALYs changed from 81,454,987.01 (UI 68,389,231.42-94,748,209.77) to 89,327,031.66 (UI 78,881,688.75-97,824,746.45).
The age-standardized CVD death rate in China changed from 496.65 (UI 420.03-571.17) to 208.03 (UI 178.08-233.08), and the age-standardized CVD DALY rate changed from 10065.67 (UI 8607.08-11541.62) to 4059.34 (UI 3593.00-4444.04).

Globally, all-age CVD deaths changed from 13,137,958.83 (UI 12,200,604.88-14,040,764.43) in 1990 to 19,139,018.09 (UI 17,355,526.66-20,403,359.76) in 2023, while all-age CVD DALYs changed from 320,141,562.84 (UI 291,872,743.51-344,484,846.93) to 436,235,457.79 (UI 401,278,571.34-464,841,017.42).
The global age-standardized CVD death rate changed from 375.51 (UI 346.66-401.06) to 214.94 (UI 194.23-229.57), and the global age-standardized CVD DALY rate changed from 8061.62 (UI 7403.37-8632.63) to 4863.82 (UI 4460.87-5192.31).

Disease-specific descriptive GBD burden analysis only. Counts and age-standardized rates must remain separate, uncertainty intervals must be retained, and no causal, clinical, mechanistic, or policy-effect claim is allowed.
